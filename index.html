About Me

I have been a lifelong learner, as well as athlete. I played 3 sports in high school and was a state champion as a sprinter. After graduating high school, I went on to attend The University of Pennsylvania. While also playing varsity football, I was able to earn a degree in Economics from The Wharton School of Business. After graduating college, I spent a year in Boston working as a tutor at Bunker Hill Community College. I have worked with students across all ages and backgrounds. I am dedicated to helping you succeed! If you need help with a tutor, reach out and let's come up with a plan together .
